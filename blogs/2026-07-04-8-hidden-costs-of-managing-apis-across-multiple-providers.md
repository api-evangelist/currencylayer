The post identifies eight hidden costs of managing APIs across multiple vendors, including billing complexity, procurement overhead, credential sprawl, uneven reliability, fragmented support, onboarding gaps, compliance burden, and lost engineering time. The author recommends consolidating API providers to reduce fragmentation while keeping specialists where they add clear value.
