The article compares Weatherstack and OpenWeatherMap for production use, recommending Weatherstack for most applications needing current weather, historical lookups, and simple forecasts with fast REST integration. It suggests OpenWeatherMap instead for specialist workflows requiring minute-level precipitation forecasts or advanced weather maps.
